Return Policy

Basic Rules

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied for any reason, you may return the item within 7 days of receiving it.

Returns Within 7 Days

All returns must be initiated within 7 days of receiving the product. Please fill out the return form below to start the return process.

Conditions for Return

The item must be unused and in the same condition as you received it. It must be in the original packaging with all tags and labels attached.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return policy, please contact our customer service team at burhadesneha96@gmail.com.

Refund Policy

We understand that circumstances may lead to the return of your purchase. In such cases, we offer a refund for the returned item. Upon approval of your return, we will refund 90% of the purchase amount. The refund will be processed through the original payment method.
